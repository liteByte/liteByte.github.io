Pax ID

Pax ID wanted a mobile app for doctors who need to log every medical attention they perform to their patients during trips. They needed a fast and reliable way of registering symptoms, diagnostics, and to see each patient's clinical history, and it needed to work both online and offline.

Our solution?

A top-notch application, tailor-made to their specific needs, which relies on a powerful backend that provides every doctor with a quick way of assisting their patients.

Technologies used:

  • Android Studio
  • Java
  • PHP
